Ques2: What are HTML entities? List out 5 commonly used HTML entities.

Sol:
The reserved characters used in HTML documents are known as HTML entities. They are not seen on the typical keyboard. They provide a wide range of characters, allowing you to add icons, geometric shapes, mathematical operators and so on.

5 commonly used HTML entities are :

1. < (less than)
entity name - &lt;
entity number - &#60;

2. & (ampersand)
entity name - &amp;
entity number - &#38;

3.copyright 
entity name - &copy;
entity number - &#169;

4.registered trademark
entity name - &reg;
entity number - &#174;

5. > (greater than)
entity name - &gt;
entity number - &#62;
